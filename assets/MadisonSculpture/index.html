About SculptSearch:


SculptSearch was created by UW-Madison students Ben Isaacs, Sammy Fogel, and David Kwong to bridge the gap between Madison's abundant public sculptures and the dearth of unified information on these sculptures. We hope this tool proves useful in your exploration of Madison's public arts.

Feel free to use the Feedback button above to provide information about any new or missing sculptures to the map. Feedback will be reviewed by an editor and added to the map if deemed appropriate.

We thank you for using SculptSearch!
